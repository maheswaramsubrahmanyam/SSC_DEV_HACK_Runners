SSC_DEV_HACK: A Remarkable Journey to Victory

2nd Prize Winners - DevXplore Hackathon 2025

Hackathons are not just competitions but also incredible learning experiences, especially for newcomers. Being the team lead of SSC_DEV_HACK, I wanted to ensure that my team members—most of whom were first-timers—understood not just the technical aspects but also the essence of collaboration, learning, and perseverance. Here's the story of how we participated, learned, grew, and emerged victorious at the DevXplore Hackathon 2k25, organized by GDG Nellore and sponsored by CAT Point.

The Journey Begins: Train Rides and Team Bonding

Our journey began on January 2nd, 2025, from Machilipatnam.

  • The train was packed, with no place to sit. While standing initially, I managed to arrange an upper berth for the girls, and I sat opposite them.
  • This train ride became the foundation of our team bonding.
  • I introduced everyone, and we spent time talking and playing the "Alphabet Game"—naming computer science-related terms.
  • Soon, the hackathon excitement took over, and we started brainstorming ideas for our project, a Virtual Shopping Assistant Recommendation System.
  • We jotted down features, processes, and modules to include, ensuring everyone's ideas were heard and noted.

Arrival at Nellore: Early Morning Hustle

We reached Nellore Railway Station at 2:45 AM on January 3rd, 2025.

  • I called Sathvik Bhayya, the event organizer, requesting accommodation for my team.
  • While waiting for the arranged rooms, we stayed at the railway station's waiting room for 2.5 hours before taking Rapido rides to the hackathon venue, Narayana Engineering College.

The Hackathon Experience

Day One: Learning, Fun, and Surprises

The day started with a classical dance performance and a keynote session by Google Developer Expert Jitendra Gupta Sir, who spoke about cloud technologies.

Project Development

Our Virtual Shopping Assistant Recommendation System included:

  • Recommendation Engine
  • Voice-Based Interaction System
  • Notification System

Challenges Along the Way

At 11:00 PM, a mentor from CAT Point reviewed our progress. Unfortunately, his feedback was disheartening, and it significantly lowered our morale. For hours, we struggled to find our footing. The team was visibly shaken, and productivity dropped until 4:00 AM.

Turning the Tide

Another mentor from CAT Point, however, reignited our spirits. He praised our problem statement, gave us valuable suggestions, and set targets to achieve. His energy and guidance motivated us, and we bounced back, completing two modules by morning.

The Magic of "Two"

  1. We started our journey on the 2nd.
  2. Reached Nellore at 2 AM.
  3. Sat in the 2nd row in the seminar hall.
  4. Worked at the 2nd table in the hackathon room.
  5. And ultimately, we secured the 2nd prize!

The Victory and Beyond

Winning 2nd Prize

We received a cash prize of ₹20,000, along with certificates and applause from the audience.

A Decision to Donate Student Service Community SSC

In a unanimous and heartfelt decision, we chose to donate the entire prize money to the Student Service Community (SSC) to support its social service activities.

Final Reflections: A Journey to Remember

This hackathon taught us invaluable lessons—not just about coding but also about teamwork, perseverance, and the power of positivity.
